About Us

Our Purpose

The CTU Buddy serves as a user-friendly platform for students, supporting academic success and enhancing the learning environment at CTU. It enables efficient timetable management, active participation in discussions, easy resource sharing, and effective peer and educator connections. Ultimately, the CTU Buddy empowers students to excel academically while fostering collaboration within the CTU community.

Our Vision

The CTU-Buddy project aims to be the preferred educational system for City Technical University students. It offers a cutting-edge platform that revolutionizes learning and peer interaction. The project envisions CTU-Buddy as a leader with innovative features, an intuitive interface, and seamless cross-device functionality. Ultimately, it strives to empower students, enhance their academic journey, and foster collaboration within the educational community.

Our Mission

The CTU-Buddy project strives to develop an inclusive and interactive educational system that enriches the learning journey for City Technical University (CTU) students. Its mission is to offer a user-friendly platform for timetable management, collaborative discussions, resource sharing, and effective peer and educator connections. Ultimately, the project aims to transform the educational experience at CTU by promoting academic achievement and creating an enhanced learning environment.
